Artistic Inspiration

As a student who has been interested in photography and visual works for many years, Man Ray has been one of my biggest inspirations for a long time, and I have even recreated some of his works for school projects.

Man Ray was a visual artist born in 1890 who spent most of his time in Paris. He created his most famous works during the 1920s.

Man Ray's works focused mostly on experimental photography, dreams, surrealism, and the human body interacting as an object.
